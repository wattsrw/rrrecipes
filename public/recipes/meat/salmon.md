## Ingredients
- 2 lbs     Salmon filets
- canola oil
- salt and pepper

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
1. Put foil sheet in baking pan. Put salmon on foil. Fold foil edges to create boat around salmon.
1. Brush oil on top and season
1. Bake for 15-20 mins
    - 15 minutes seems to work best
