## Ingredients
- Turkey breast roast
- Oil
- Seasoning

## Directions
1. Heat oven to **350** degrees
1. Mix oil and seasoning all over turkey
    - **Do not** remove mesh casing
1. Place turkey on pan and in oven
1. Cook for **30 minutes per pound** of meat
1. Remove turkey from oven, cover with foil, and let rest for 10 minutes
