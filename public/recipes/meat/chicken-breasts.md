## Ingredients
- Chicken breasts
- Oil
- Seasoning

## Directions
1. Mix oil and seasoning all over chicken
1. Heat oil in skillet at medium-high heat
1. Place chicken in skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
    - Try with top side down
1. Flip chicken
1. Turn heat to low and cover
1. Cook for 10-15 minutes
    - 15 minutes works best
