## Ingredients
- Heavy cream
    - 3 cups
- Whole milk
    - 1 cup
- Sugar
    - 3/4 cup
- Vanilla extract
    - 2 tablespoons
- Egg yolks
    - 4

## Directions
1. Separate the 4 egg yolks into a bowl and whisk.
1. Heat cream, milk, and sugar in saucepan. Stir occassionally until sugar is dissolved and mixture is hot.
1. Pour egg yolks into hot mixture.
1. Cook over **medium heat**, stirring constantly, until mixture thickens and coats back of a spoon
    - Don't want it to boil
    - Takes about **6 minutes**
1. Strain the mixture into a sealable container.
1. Add vanilla extract.
1. Refrigerate for at least **2 hours**
1. Pour mixture into machine. Start.

## Wait Times
- Ice cream mixture gets stored in refrigerator for at least **2 hours** before being put into machine

## Things to Try
- Maybe try heating the mixture over **medium-low** heat and cook a little longer (8 minutes)
    - At medium heat, it started to boil after 6 minutes
