## Ingredients
- Ground beef
    - 2 lbs 
- Tomatoes
    - 8
- Onion
    - 1
- Cinnamon
    - 1 tablespoon
- Chili seasoning mix
    - 4-5 tablespoon
- Dark chocolate
    - 6 pieces
- Shredded cheddar cheese
- Noodles

## Directions
1. Dice tomatoes and onion. Put into pot.
1. Cook ground beef in skillet. When finished separate from oil and put into pot.
1. Add cinnamon, chicli seasoning, and chocolate into pot. 
1. Mix ingredients together while bringing mixture to a boil.
1. Once boiling, cover pot with lid and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
1. Cook noodles in separate pot.
1. Continue cooking chili and stirring without lid until desired consistency.
