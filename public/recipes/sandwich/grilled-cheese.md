## Ingredients
- Cheese
- Mayo
- Bread

## Directions
1. Slice cheese
1. Apply Mayo on outside of each bread slice
1. Assemble sandwich
1. Heat skillet to medium-low
1. Put sandwich into skillet and cover
1. Cook for about 4 mintues
1. Flip sandwich and cover again
1. Turn heat to low
1. Cook for another 4 minutes
