## Ingredients
- Unsalted butter
    - 3/4 cup 
- Sugar
    - 1/2 cup 
- Light brown sugar
    - 1/2 cup 
- Egg
    - 1
- Vanilla extract
    - 1 teaspoons 
- Bananas
    - 2
- Flour
    - 2 cups 
- Salt
    - 3/4 teaspoon 
- Baking soda
    - 1 teaspoon 
- Cinnamon
    - 1/2 teaspoon
- Old-fashioned oats
    - 1 cup
- Chocolate chips
    - 1.5 cups

## Directions
1. Combine flour, oats, baking soda, salt, cinnamon until combined in big bowl.
1. Cream together butter and sugars until combined in medium bowl.
1. Beat in eggs and vanilla until light in same medium bowl.
1. Mix in mashed bananas into same medium bowl
1. Mix in wet ingredients into dry ingrdients.
    - If the dough appears too sticky, add 1 to 2 tablesppons (up to 1/4 cup) of flour
1. Add chocolate chips and mix well.
1. Cookie dough can be refrigerated **30 minutes to 24 hours**
    - Up to a couple days
1. Preheat oven to **350 degrees**. 
1. Line baking sheets with parchment paper and set aside.
1. Roll **2 tablespoons of dough** at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.
1. Bake in oven for approximately **11-12 minutes**. Take them out when they are just barely starting to turn brown.
    - If coming from fridge, cook time is closer to **14-16 minutes**
1. Let them sit on the baking pan for 5 minutes before removing to cooling rack.

## Wait Times
- **1-2 hours** before starting for butter to soften
- Dough can be refrigerated before baking
