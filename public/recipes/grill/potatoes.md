## Ingredients
- Potatoes
- Oil
- Seasoning

## Directions
1. Heat grill to medium-high heat (400-450 degrees)
1. Wash potatoes
1. Poke holes in potatoes with fork
1. Cover potatoes in oil and seasoning
1. Wrap in foil
    - Not large potatoes can be wrapped all together
1. Place on top rack of grill. Cook for 60 minutes
