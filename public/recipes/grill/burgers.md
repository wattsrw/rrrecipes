## Ingredients
- Ground beef
- Salt and pepper
- Cheese

## Directions
1. Heat grill on **medium-high heat (450 degrees)**
1. Season ground beef
1. Separate ground beef into **1/3 pound patties**
1. Fold cheese corners in
1. Put patties onto grill and cook **4 mins** each side
    - put cheese on about **1 minute** before done cooking
