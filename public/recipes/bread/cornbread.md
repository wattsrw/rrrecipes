## Ingredients
- Milk
    - 1.25 cups
- Unsalted butter
    - 6 tablespoons
- Eggs
    - 2
- All-purpose flour
    - 1.5 cups
- Yellow cornmeal (not self-rising)
    - 1 cup
- Granulated sugar
    - 1 cup
- Baking powder
    - 1 teaspoon
- Baking Soda
    - 1 teaspoon
- Salt
    - 1/2 teaspoon

## Directions
1. Beat eggs
1. Add all ingredients to bucket
1. Put bucket into bread machine
1. Menu option **#10 (Cake)**
    - Dark color
    - Largest loaf size
1. Start machine
1. Let cool in bucket for a bit
1. Use spatula to separate bread from bucket sides
1. Cool on wire rack for at least an hour

## Wait Times
- Optional **1-2 hours** before starting for butter to soften
